London's Most Instagrammable Cafés

FOOD

by THE PREMIUM LIFESTYLE Team, 23 February 2021

Balifornia

Organic, vegan, healthy(-ish?). The myth that vegan food is boring is long gone. Balifornia has the most stunning decor and makes us wonder if we’re still in London or somehow got magically teleported to a beach club in Bali. We recommend going on a sunny day, to get the perfect lighting for your Instagram shot.

The Cupcake Factory

You can smell the aim of cupcakes as you are getting closer to Rose Street. The Cupcake Factory's flower cupcakes are a work of art and will guarantee you plenty of likes on your IG post. Can't decide on one cupcake? Take two, or three, and bring some home! The cupcakes are perfect for birthdays, weddings, and parties too.

Pinkposh

Ah, Pinkposh. The Insta favorite doesn't require an introduction. Search for London on Instagram, and you'll find Pinkposh. What color do you want your latte - blue, pink, or green? With their pink interior, your feed will look like the one of a top influencer.

Chelsea Café

Tea anyone? The classic Chelsea Café is always a safe bet, with its lovely staff and delicious British food. They are known for their afternoon tea and champagne. The place is perfect for people-watching, and getting a short glimpse into the life of the Chelsea locals.

Sketch

Sketch. The one and only. The legendary. As famous for their pink interior, as for their Michellin food. Oh, and don’t forget to take a photo when you visit their eggshaped bathrooms.
